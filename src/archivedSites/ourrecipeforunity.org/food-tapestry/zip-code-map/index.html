Come Explore our Community!

Welcome to our Food Tapestry℠ map, where you can see the breadth of Our Recipe for Unity’s℠ community. Each pin represents the ZIP Code of a Unifier who has submitted at least one recipe or Food Connection Story to our collection.

Do you wonder what people in your area have provided to Our Recipe for Unity℠? Are you curious about what cuisines are being cooked around you, and what connections are waiting to be made? We encourage you to search through our Recipes and Food Connection Stories, and we hope that what you find inspires you to submit a Recipe or Food Connection Story. By becoming a Unifier, you can claim your spot on the map!

Your information is private; Our Recipe for Unity℠ will never give away, trade or sell your name or location. See our Privacy Policy for more information.

To use
Simply click the on-screen tools to zoom in and out; click and drag the map to move.
Click pins to get more information and linked recipes and stories.
